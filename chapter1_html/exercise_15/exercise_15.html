23 June 2015 | Archive

JTC 1, Information Technology of the
International Organization for Standardization
(ISO) and the International Electrotechnical
Commission (IEC), announced approval of the
MathML Version 3.0 2nd Edition as an ISO/IEC
International Standard (ISO/IEC 40314:2015).
